Iced Americano recipe


Description

This is an iced coffee drink that is really popular in South Korea. Mine is a simpler variant I make from instant coffee but it still tastes amazing

Ingredients

  • Instant coffee
  • Iced water
  • Room temp water
  • Sugar or honey (obtional)

This is how you make it

  1. Take the instant coffee and put in a large glass
  2. Add the room temp water (1/10 max of the glass)
  3. If you wish to you can add any sweetener of your choice. I prefer it bitter tho
  4. Mix well using a spoon
  5. Fill the glass with iced water, and enjoy your delicious Iced Americano
