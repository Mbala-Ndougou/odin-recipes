Peanut Butter recipe


Description

This is my absolute favorite snack. It's a hundred percent natural and healthy, but make sure not to become addicted as it is really high in calories

Ingredients

To make this, we'll need

  • Peanuts
  • Honey
  • Salt

It's really easy to do:

  1. Grind the peanuts with a blender. I like to keep some bits in mine for texture but you can grind it to full smoothness if you want
  2. Add a pinch of salt and a scoop of honey
  3. Mix with a spoon and put in a container of your choice.
